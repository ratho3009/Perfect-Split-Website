Privacy Policy & App Support

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information I Collect

Perfect Split is designed to respect your privacy. However, to provide certain features, I use third-party services that may collect data:

  • Leaderboards (Firebase): If you choose to submit a score, I collect and store your chosen nickname and score to display on the global leaderboard.
  • Advertising (AdMob): I use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS or Advertising ID on Android) and usage data to provide personalized advertising.

2. How I Use Your Information

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To maintain and display the global high score leaderboard.
  • To serve advertisements that keep the game free to play.

3. Data Deletion & Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your leaderboard data at any time. If you want your nickname and score removed from my database, please contact me using the support information below.

4. Children's Privacy

Perfect Split does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided me with personal data, please contact me so I can delete it.
